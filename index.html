Welcome to Hillside Paradise

Welcome To Our Farmhouse

Welcome to HillSideParadise, your serene escape nestled in the picturesque landscape of Maldunge, just a short 20-minute drive from Panvel. Situated at the foot of the majestic Matheran mountains, HillSideParadise offers a tranquil retreat surrounded by lush greenery, where you can truly connect with nature.

As you step onto the grounds of HillSideParadise, you'll immediately feel the stress of the outside world melt away. The sprawling farmstead is a haven of peace and tranquility, away from the hustle and bustle of city life. The verdant surroundings create an atmosphere of serenity, making it the perfect destination for a rejuvenating getaway.

One of the highlights of HillSideParadise is its natural stream, which flows gracefully through the property, providing a soothing soundtrack to your stay. During the monsoon season, the stream comes to life, offering the perfect opportunity to splash around and immerse yourself in the refreshing waters.

For those seeking relaxation and leisure, HillSideParadise features a well-maintained swimming pool, where you can take a refreshing dip or simply lounge by the water's edge, soaking up the sun's warmth. Whether you're spending time with family or catching up with friends, the pool provides a delightful respite from the heat.

At HillSideParadise, you can leave the worries of the outside world behind and immerse yourself in the beauty of nature. Whether you're exploring the verdant surroundings, taking a dip in the natural stream, or lounging by the pool, you'll find endless opportunities to unwind and recharge. So pack your bags, gather your loved ones, and escape to HillSideParadise for a day of pure bliss and relaxation.
